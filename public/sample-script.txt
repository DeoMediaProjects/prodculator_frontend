FADE IN:

INT. NEW YORK APARTMENT - DAY

SARAH (30s, determined) sits at her laptop in a cramped Manhattan apartment. The skyline of New York City is visible through the window.

                    SARAH
          I can't keep doing this. Not in 
          this city.

She closes her laptop and looks out at the cityscape.

EXT. NEW YORK CITY STREETS - DAY

Sarah walks through the crowded streets of Manhattan, passing Times Square. The energy of New York pulses around her.

INT. COFFEE SHOP - NEW YORK - DAY

Sarah meets JAMES (40s, weathered) in a busy coffee shop near Central Park.

                    JAMES
          You really think leaving New York 
          is the answer?

                    SARAH
          Sometimes you need to leave to 
          find yourself.

EXT. CENTRAL PARK - DAY

They walk through Central Park. The Manhattan skyline looms in the background.

INT. SARAH'S APARTMENT - BROOKLYN - NIGHT

Sarah packs her belongings in her Brooklyn apartment. Photos of New York landmarks cover the walls.

EXT. JFK AIRPORT - DAY

Sarah drags her suitcase through the departures area of John F. Kennedy International Airport.

INT. AIRPLANE - DAY

Sarah looks out the window as New York City disappears below the clouds.

EXT. LOS ANGELES AIRPORT - DAY

Sarah emerges into the bright California sunshine at LAX.

INT. LOS ANGELES HOTEL ROOM - NIGHT

Sarah unpacks in a modest hotel room. The Hollywood sign is visible in the distance.

EXT. VENICE BEACH - LOS ANGELES - DAY

Sarah walks along the beach, taking in the California coast. Street performers and artists fill the boardwalk.

INT. COFFEE SHOP - LOS ANGELES - DAY

Sarah sits alone with her laptop in a trendy LA coffee shop in Silver Lake.

EXT. HOLLYWOOD BOULEVARD - NIGHT

Sarah walks past the Chinese Theatre and the Walk of Fame. Los Angeles at night is electric but different from New York.

INT. PRODUCTION OFFICE - LOS ANGELES - DAY

Sarah enters a sleek production office in Burbank. Modern and professional.

EXT. GRIFFITH OBSERVATORY - LOS ANGELES - SUNSET

Sarah stands at Griffith Observatory, looking out over the sprawling city of Los Angeles as the sun sets.

INT. NEW RESTAURANT - LOS ANGELES - NIGHT

Sarah has dinner alone in a trendy Downtown LA restaurant. She pulls out her phone and stares at it.

EXT. SANTA MONICA PIER - DAY

Sarah walks to the end of the Santa Monica Pier. The Pacific Ocean stretches endlessly before her.

INT. SARAH'S NEW APARTMENT - LOS ANGELES - DAY

Sarah unpacks boxes in her new apartment in Los Angeles. It's brighter, more spacious than her New York place.

EXT. RUNYON CANYON - LOS ANGELES - DAY

Sarah hikes in Runyon Canyon with new friends. The Hollywood sign is visible on the hill.

INT. PRODUCTION STUDIO - LOS ANGELES - DAY

Sarah works confidently in a large production studio. She's found her place.

EXT. ROOFTOP BAR - LOS ANGELES - NIGHT

Sarah meets friends on a rooftop bar in Downtown LA. The Los Angeles skyline glitters around them.

INT. SARAH'S APARTMENT - LOS ANGELES - NIGHT

Sarah video calls with James. New York feels like a lifetime ago.

                    JAMES
          How's California treating you?

                    SARAH
          Los Angeles isn't New York... 
          and that's exactly what I needed.

EXT. SANTA MONICA BEACH - SUNSET

Sarah watches the sun set over the Pacific Ocean. She's finally home.

FADE OUT.
